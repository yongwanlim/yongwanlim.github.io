Yongwan Lim

Research Scientist, University of Southern California

I am a researcher in Electrical and Computer Engineering in the Viterbi School of Engineering at the University of Southern California (USC) where I also serve as staff scientist at Dynamic Imaging Science Center.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from USC in December 2020. Since starting my Ph.D. study, I have worked closely with Dr. Krishna S. Nayak (co-advisor), Dr. Shrikanth Narayanan (co-advisor), and Dr. Dani Byrd in the Magnetic Resonance Engineerining Lab (MREL), Signal Analysis and Interpretation Lab (SAIL), and Speech Production and Articulation kNowledge (SPAN) group at USC.

My research interests are broadly in developing novel computational imaging and post-processing techniques based on signal processing, mathematical optimization, and machine and deep learning.
